Jennie Kim

A pretty and talented Korean idol

The Journey of Kim Jennie's Life:

  • 1996: Born in Cheongdam-dong, Gangnam-gu, Seoul.
  • 2006: Featured on MBC's documentary English, Must Change to Survive.
  • 2010: Auditioned and succeeded in being a trainee for YG Entertainment.
  • 2016: She debuted as member of Blackpink.
  • 2018: Due to the popularity of Blackpink she have been invited to a lot of Korean variety shows.
  • 2019: Became the first Korean solo artist to perform at Coachella.
  • 2023: Made her acting debut in the HBO television series The Idol.
